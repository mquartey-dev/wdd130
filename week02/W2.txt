- HTML is used for structure, not to style pages
- 2 types of tags: Semantic and Non-Semantic
- Semantic tags have meaning built into their name
- Non-Semantic tags are the opposite
- Common examples: div - A container used for grouping and styling a section of a page
                    span - A container used for styling small portions of text

- CSS(Cascading Style Sheets) is a rule set language that defines the style and presentation pf an HTML document
